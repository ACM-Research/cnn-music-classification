Music Genre Classification

Created by Agastya Bose, Ethan Emmanuel, Jack Myrick, Zayne Lumpkin, & Megan Vu for ACM Research at UT Dallas

This is a web-interface to a convolutional neural network trained to classify audio into musical genres. You may upload a file directly, or submit a YouTube link to a video featuring the sound to analyze. The sample must be at least three seconds long, and response times will increase with audio length.
